Importance of CSS

CSS is important in the design of a webpage because it controls all design-related aspects of a website such as topography, colours, page layouts and any other visual aspects of a website. it also creates a great user experience.

Difference between paddings, margins and borders

Padding is the inner space between the content and the border of your box

Border is the perimeter of the box, it can be invisible or it could be a thick colored line.

Margin is the outer space surrounding the box i.e the space between the border and outer elements.
